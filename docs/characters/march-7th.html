    Abilities

    Basic ATK (Single Target) | Frigid Cold Arrow | Deals Ice DMG equal to 50%–130% of March 7th's ATK to a single enemy.

    Skill (Defence) | The Power of Cuteness | Provides a single ally with a Shield that can absorb DMG equal to 38%–66.5% of March 7th's DEF plus 190–973.75 for 3 turn(s). If the ally's current HP percentage is 30% or higher, greatly increases the chance of enemies attacking that ally.

    Ultimate (AoE) | Glacial Cascade | Deals Ice DMG equal to 90%–180% of March 7th's ATK to all enemies. Hit enemies have a 50% base chance to be Frozen for 1 turn(s). While Frozen, enemies cannot take action and will receive Additional Ice DMG equal to 30%–75% of March 7th's ATK at the beginning of each turn.
